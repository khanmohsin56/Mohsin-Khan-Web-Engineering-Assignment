About PAF-IAST

Transforming Pakistan's Youth into a National Dividend

Excellence in Applied Sciences & Technology

Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a premier public science and technology university located in Mang, Haripur, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

Vision: PAF-IAST is a collaborative venture with select Austrian and Chinese Universities, established to elevate the quality of technical education in Pakistan through skill training and human resource development.

Location: Our purpose-built campus is located approximately 40 miles from Islamabad, nestled in a scenic rural, mountainous area with a naturally formed lake in the heart of the campus.

Inauguration: The foundation stone was laid on May 7, 2016, and the university was officially inaugurated by Prime Minister Imran Khan on September 17, 2020.

Our Campus

A Purpose-Built Environment for Learning and Innovation

PAF-IAST features a state-of-the-art campus located 10 km south of Haripur and 60 km north of Islamabad. The campus is set in a scenic mountainous area with a natural lake, providing an ideal environment for academic excellence.
